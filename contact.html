Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn coins?

You earn coins by completing quizzes. The number of coins depends on your performance - the more questions you answer correctly, the more coins you earn!

Do I need to create an account?

No! SmartQuizHub works as a guest platform. Your progress and coins are saved locally on your device, so you can start playing immediately.

Can I retake quizzes?

Yes, you can retake any quiz as many times as you want. However, you'll only earn coins the first time you complete each quiz.

What can I do with my coins?

Currently, coins serve as a measure of your achievement. We're working on a rewards shop where you'll be able to unlock themes, power-ups, and other exciting features!
